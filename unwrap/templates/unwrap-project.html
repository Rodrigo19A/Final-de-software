The Computer Component Project

We grew up with outdated ideas about computer components!

Our performance and efficiency are at risk. Old parts are limiting.

Every component ever manufactured is still in use, either on outdated systems, in e-waste landfills, or even in the devices we rely on daily. The chips, the motherboards, the memory — all of these are part of the ecosystem that we need to update to keep up with modern computing demands.

So let's upgrade and reduce hardware limitations!

Our goal is to make transitioning to the latest technology accessible to everyone. We offer alternatives to upgrade your systems with the best, most efficient components available.

We want to keep it easy, efficient, and cost-effective. Our first step is to offer custom PC builds, featuring locally sourced components with a focus on quality, performance, and sustainability. Our ultimate goal is to open a physical store for tech enthusiasts and professionals looking for the latest in hardware solutions.

Our Partners

We’ve partnered with leading manufacturers to bring high-quality, performance-driven components to the market. Our curated suppliers focus on providing durable, high-tech components that deliver unmatched performance.

Our partners are chosen based on 4 key criteria:

  • High-performance components
  • Exceptional quality and durability
  • Energy-efficient and environmentally conscious
  • Locally sourced or major industry leaders
